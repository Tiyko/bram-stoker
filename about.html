About Website Author

My name is Ionut (Romanian for Johnny), and this website's purpose is to educate and give an introduction to, the man who gave us Dracula, Bram Stoker. It brings together his life as novelist writter, career in theatre and the commemoration that we have today in Dublin, Ireland on Halloween know as Bram Stoker Festival.

Credit and Research

To put this website together I have made a research on public domain's available data about Bram stoker such as Wikipedia, Wikisource and The Victorian Web for information about his life. I have used news websites like The Irish Times and RTE to quote and insipre from key paragraphs important to highlight Bram Stoker Festival.

The pictures I have used on the website are all picked from public domain websites or users of Flickr who have chosed to have their work out on public domain and I am grateful that they did so as it allowed me to give the website a more pleasant and interactive look and feel. The websites used are Wikimedia Commons, Openverse (as a search engine), Flickr and Public Domain Pictures for the largest part. All pictures are connected to their original location with clickable links to give credit to their authors and also a full view of the pictures.
