About Bram Stoker Festival

Terms & Conditions

Age Suitability

Due to mature themes and some strong language, the content of some Bram Stoker Festival shows may not be viewed as suitable for all audiences. Shows that are suitable for children will be labelled as such on our website.

International Patrons

Many of this year's shows can be enjoyed from anywhere in the world! Book your tickets via our website, or take part in many of our free events from the comfort of your own home.

Accessibility

We aim to make our programme as accessible as possible. This includes captioning for some of our digital content for deaf / hard of hearing viewers. Please check individual event pages or contact info@bramstokerfestival for further information.

Box Office

Please check individual event pages for information on ticketed shows. Tickets can be purchased online or by calling +353 1 518 0599. Our Box Office provider is TakeYourSeats for all ticketed events except Dracula's Disco - tickets for this event, when available, can be secured via The Ark www.theark.ie.

Event Start Times

For online film and digital events, the film / show will start at the advertised time - please ensure you log on in plenty of time as it will not be possible to start later than the advertised start time.
